Leisure Suit Larry

is a text-based adventure game released by Sierra many moons ago. It’s the only text adventure that Sierra released and is the precursor to the Leisure Suit Larry series.

The basic controls are E, N, S, W, U and D. Type INVENTORY to see what you’re carrying. At the top of the screen you can see where you can go and what you can interact with at the given location. You begin the game with $2,500 and can always go to the casino to try and win more money. Exact commands you need to type are in capitals.

Casino
